What's in a Reputation Score?

The Score represents an Internet user's overall perspective of your business on review sites.

The top Score of 1000 is reserved for businesses with perfect reviews and high volumes of taffic.
Above 900 is considered exceptional and would represent an elevated status among your peers.

Nat'l Avg.

233

The Score is calculated by analyzing the components below:

Star Average

Your star average measures your average rating, applying factors like the popularity of review sites and review quality to each review.

Length

Consumers perceive long reviews to be more accurate and authoritative than short reviews.

Volume

The more reviews a location has, the more it will be perceived as an established business by the community.

Spread

Reviews should be distributed across multiple sites, and the distribution should match the importance of each review site.

Recentness

Recent reviews create a fresh and vibrant feel for a location.

Visibility

This rating measures your review presence on the most visible review sites.
